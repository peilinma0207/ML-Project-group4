This dataset contains TED and TED-Ed video clips related to artificial intelligence, machine learning, deep learning, AI agents, misinformation, and AI understanding.

Each sample includes:
- one selected video segment
- one extracted audio file
- one ground truth transcript
- metadata describing the source, speaker, topic, and selected time range

The RAG documents provide background knowledge for transcript repair. They are intended to help the agent recognize technical terms, speaker names, AI-related concepts, and topic-specific expressions.

The dataset source is TED and TED-Ed, not YouTube. The collected videos are centered around artificial intelligence and machine learning topics.
