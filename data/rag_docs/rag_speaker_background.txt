Andrew Ng is a computer scientist and machine learning educator known for his work in artificial intelligence, deep learning, and online education. He co-founded Coursera and has taught machine learning to a large global audience.

Eric Schmidt is a technology executive and former CEO of Google. He often discusses artificial intelligence, technological change, and the social impact of AI.

Jeremy Howard is a data scientist and entrepreneur known for his work in deep learning, AI education, and practical machine learning.

D. Scott Phoenix discusses human-AI interaction and the possibility of humans merging with artificial intelligence technologies.

Peter Steinberger is a software developer and entrepreneur. In the TED talk used in this dataset, he discusses OpenClaw and AI coding agents.

Keith Coleman and Jay Baxter are associated with Community Notes, a crowdsourced system designed to add context to online information and reduce misinformation.

Briana Brownell explains how artificial intelligence systems learn, including supervised learning and reinforcement learning.

Charles Wallace and Dan Kwartler discuss the Chinese Room thought experiment and whether AI systems truly understand language.
